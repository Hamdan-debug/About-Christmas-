Christmas Traditions

Many customs and traditions bring Christmas to life, such as decorating Christmas trees, exchanging gifts, singing carols, and preparing festive meals. Santa Claus, based on Saint Nicholas, plays a significant role, bringing gifts to children around the world. In addition, lighting candles and hanging wreaths symbolize hope and togetherness.Everybody around the world celebrates it !!!!!
